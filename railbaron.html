Rail Baron Tools

Decide amongst your group who is going first and what order you're using.

Select your number of players and hit go, assign player 1 to the first box, player 2 to the second, etc.

Alternatively, you can let players draft a starting position, that sounds fun!

Information areas are available to fill in color and name of each player for personal reference.

If you just want to query the payoff chart for a specific route, select "Calculator".
